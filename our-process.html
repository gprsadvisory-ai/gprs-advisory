Our Process

Value is created before and during the process — not at the end. Every GPRS Advisory engagement follows a disciplined four-phase framework designed to maximise outcome, maintain confidentiality and deliver certainty of completion.

Phase 1
Strategic Assessment & Transaction Readiness
Estimated duration: 2–4 weeks

The preparation phase is where outcomes are determined. We invest the time required to understand the business deeply — identifying value drivers, addressing issues before buyers price them in, and building the strategic positioning that defines what the process can achieve.

By the time the first counterparty sees the business, the work that protects and maximises value is already done.

Key Activities

  • Quality of earnings and financial normalisation
  • Revenue, margin and working capital analysis
  • Commercial, operational and risk review
  • Value-enhancement and risk-mitigation planning
  • Preliminary valuation guidance and key sensitivities
  • Indicative counterparty universe and outreach strategy
  • Transaction roadmap and proposed timetable
Phase 2
Market Preparation
Estimated duration: 4–6 weeks

Credibility is established before the first conversation. We prepare the equity story, financial presentation and diligence documentation that give sophisticated counterparties the confidence to engage — and the information to move decisively.

Key Deliverables

  • Confidential information memorandum (IM)
  • One-page teaser (non-identifying)
  • Financial summary pack including agreed normalisations
  • Target counterparty list and contact strategy
  • NDA workflow and controlled release protocols
  • Data room structure and index
Phase 3
Engagement & Offers
Estimated duration: 6–8 weeks

Competitive tension does not happen by accident. It is engineered — through selective counterparty engagement, structured bid timelines and disciplined process governance that keeps qualified buyers moving simultaneously toward a decision.

We manage that process entirely. The business operates. The outcome improves.

Key Activities

  • Targeted outreach and qualification
  • NDA management and staged information release
  • Management meetings and site visit coordination
  • Indicative offer management and bid process governance
  • Negotiation support on price, structure and key terms
Phase 4
Execution & Completion
Estimated duration: 8–12 weeks

Once a preferred counterparty is confirmed, we manage the full execution phase — coordinating due diligence, driving documentation and maintaining deal momentum through to settlement.

Senior attention does not diminish as the process approaches completion. It intensifies.

Key Activities

  • Due diligence coordination and project management
  • Liaison with legal, tax and accounting advisers
  • Issue management and deal preservation
  • Final negotiation and documentation support
  • Completion and transition coordination

Total indicative timeline: 7–10 months from engagement to completion, subject to conditions, counterparty responsiveness and transaction complexity.
