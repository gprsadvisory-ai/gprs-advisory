Expertise

Capital Partnering

Growth capital. Partial liquidity. Strategic partners.

Not every transaction is a full exit. For businesses seeking growth capital, partial liquidity or a strategic partner, we identify and engage the right institutional capital — on terms that preserve founder intent and operational control.

What We Do
  • Capital strategy and structure design — growth equity, minority stake, majority with retained interest
  • Equity story and financial narrative preparation
  • Investor identification — private equity, family offices, long-term capital partners, search funds
  • Targeted investor engagement and process management
  • Term sheet negotiation and documentation support
  • Coordination with legal, tax and accounting advisers through completion
The right capital changes what is possible.

Many founders reach a point where they want to access capital, share risk or bring in a strategic partner — without selling the business outright.

The right partner can accelerate growth, provide liquidity and add capability, while leaving the founder in control.

Finding that partner requires the same process discipline as a full sale — the right targeting, the right positioning and the right structure. We bring all three.

Capital We Engage

The capital we work with.

Private Equity

Structured growth capital

Structured capital, scalable growth businesses, active acquirers. Suited to businesses seeking institutional backing with a defined growth or exit pathway.

Family Offices

Patient, long-term capital

Long-term capital, relationship-driven, legacy-aware. Often the right partner for founders who want to retain involvement and grow at their own pace.

Strategic Acquirers

Synergy-focused partners

Sector specialists, premium for strategic fit. Can offer more than capital — market access, operational capability and brand alignment.

Search Funds

Operator-investors

Founder-friendly transition, simple recurring revenue. Suited to founders seeking an engaged successor who will run and grow the business.
