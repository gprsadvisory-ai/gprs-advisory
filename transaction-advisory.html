Four Points of Failure

Where value is lost.

01 — Before Market

Underprepared at launch

Businesses reach market without financial normalisation, strategic positioning or risk identification. Buyers price in uncertainty before they even engage.

02 — Buyer Selection

Wrong buyers, no tension

Wrong buyers are approached. No institutional capital. No competitive tension. The seller negotiates against themselves.

03 — Process Control

Buyers control the timeline

No structured bidding, no deadline pressure. Buyers drag timelines and chip the price throughout the process.

04 — Due Diligence

Late-stage surprises

Issues emerge in DD that were never pre-empted. Price retrades or deal collapse at the final stage.

How We Recover It

A four-step process that protects value.

STEP 01
Preparation

Financial normalisation, risk identification, strategic positioning. We address issues before buyers price them in — and build the equity story that underpins the process.

STEP 02
Buyer Strategy

Curated outreach, not public listings. Private equity, family offices, strategic acquirers and search funds — targeted to mandate fit. The right buyers, not more buyers.

STEP 03
Controlled Process

Multiple buyers engaged simultaneously. Structured bidding. Controlled timelines. Price is engineered through competition — not negotiated under pressure.

STEP 04
Execution

Due diligence management, documentation support, completion coordination. Senior execution throughout — protecting value to the final signature.
