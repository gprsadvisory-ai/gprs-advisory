George founded GPRS Advisory to bring institutional process discipline and genuine senior attention to mid-market transactions. With 15+ years of experience across M&A advisory, corporate finance and private capital, he has advised founders, families and investors on transactions, ownership transitions and capital partnerships across a broad range of industries and transaction types.

Our Philosophy

Senior-led.
No hand-offs.

The adviser you meet at the start of an engagement is the adviser who executes your transaction. We do not hand off to junior teams once a mandate is signed.

Every client receives direct, ongoing access to senior advisory capability — from first conversation to completion. We deliberately limit the number of mandates we carry so each engagement receives the attention it deserves.

Principal-level execution
Every mandate is managed at principal level throughout. No hand-offs. No exceptions.
Deliberate capacity limits
We take on fewer mandates so each one receives the attention it deserves.
Genuine commitment to outcome
Our fee structure means our success is tied directly to yours. We win when you win.
