Succession & Transition Planning

Plan ahead. Protect what you have built.

Ownership transitions are among the most consequential decisions a founder or family will make. The difference between a good outcome and a great one is almost always preparation — and how far in advance that preparation begins.

What We Do
  • Succession strategy and ownership pathway mapping
  • Business valuation guidance and key sensitivity analysis
  • Value-building roadmap — identifying and addressing gaps that impact valuation
  • Key-person risk mitigation — management depth, documented processes, retention planning
  • Ownership structure review and transition planning
  • Management buyout advisory and structuring
  • Pre-transaction financial, operational and governance preparation
  • Coordination with legal, accounting and tax advisers
The best transactions start long before the process.

Businesses that enter a sale or transition already prepared — clean financials, documented systems, reduced key-person risk, clear strategic narrative — consistently achieve better valuations, better terms and higher certainty of completion.

Sooner is better.

Preparation is not a phase of the transaction process. It is the foundation on which outcomes are built. We work with founders at that stage — addressing value gaps, strengthening management depth and building the positioning that determines what is possible when the time comes.

Start the conversation.

Whether you have a transaction in mind or are simply thinking ahead, we are happy to have a confidential discussion about where your business stands and what the options look like.
